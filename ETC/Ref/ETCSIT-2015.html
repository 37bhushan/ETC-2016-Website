ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Department of Computer Engineering, K. K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education & Research, Nashik is organizing an International Conference on "Emerging Trends in Computer Engineering, Science and Information Technology 2015"(ETCSIT-2015). ETCSIT-2015 is a platform to bring together Professionals, academic researchers with a view to present and discuss conceptual and experimental work. The conference aims to provide a forum for eminent academicians, technologists, scientists and researchers to exchange their ideas on the latest developments and future trends in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. ETCSIT-2015 also provides a platform for UG and PG students & encourage them to present their work based on final year project only.
