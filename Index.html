ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Department of Computer Engineering, K. K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education & Research, Nashik has organized International Conference on “Emerging Trends in Computing 2016”(ETC-2016). ETC-2016 is a platform to bring together Professionals, academic researchers with a view to present and discuss conceptual and experimental work. The conference aims to provide a forum to exchange the ideas on latest developments in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. ETC-2016 invites original, unpublished and quality research papers..

CONFERENCE TOPICS

Researchers are encouraged to submit original research contributions in the following primary areas and their ancillary components (but not limited to).

 

  • Big Data And Analytics
  • Business Intelligence
  • Data Warehousing, Mining
  • Internet Of Things
  • Social Networks
  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Speech Processing
  • Image Processing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Information Retrieval
  • Software Engineering
  • Swarm Intelligence
  • Computer Networks & Security
  • Computer Graphics
  • Parallel And Distributed Computing
  • Machine Learning
  • Multimedia Applications
  • Green Computing
